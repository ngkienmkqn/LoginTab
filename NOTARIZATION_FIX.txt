MAC OS FIX (Gatekeeper - "App is damaged/cannot be verified")
============================================================

If you see an error saying "Login Tab" is damaged or cannot be opened:

1. Open 'Terminal' (Cmd + Space, type Terminal).
2. Copy and paste the following command:

   xattr -cr /Applications/Login\ Tab.app

3. Press Enter.
4. Open the App again. It should work now!
